Our Story

Founded in 1995, Victoria Hotel has been a landmark of luxury and comfort in Uganda for over 25 years. Named after the magnificent Lake Victoria, we pride ourselves on offering world-class hospitality with a distinct Ugandan charm.


Our Mission

To provide our guests with an unforgettable experience through exceptional service, luxurious accommodations, and a commitment to sustainability.
